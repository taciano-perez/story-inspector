Story Inspector

Story Inspector is a new book analysis tool that uses Artificial Intelligence to automatically extract information about works of fiction. It provides authors with insights about their manuscripts in order to improve their craft.

Story Inspector is published under the GPLv2 license, meaning that you can download and use it for free.

Download Story Inspector

Features

Book summary, including chapters, characters and locations.

Analysis of emotions and sentiments.

 

Readability score per page.

Report on the variety of sentence lengths.
